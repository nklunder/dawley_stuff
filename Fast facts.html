Annual Award Nominations Due January 9th

  

Do you know of a business or person who made a significant contribution to the visitor industry this year? Please consider submitting a nomination to KVB's Awards Committee. Winners will be announced at the Annual Awards Banquet on Friday, February 27, 2015. Click here for a nominations form and description of each award. The deadline to submit a nomination is Friday, January 9, 2015.

  Membership Luncheon January 16th  

Join us Friday, January 16th at 12 noon at the Cape Fox Lodge for our January membership luncheon. Guest speaker will be Joe Jacobson, Economic Development Director for the State of Alaska. Menu for the luncheon is Prosciutto Wrapped Chicken with Pesto sauce over Alfredo. A vegetarian option is available with advance RSVP. Cost is $18 per person when you RSVP by Monday January 12th and jumps to $22 for reservations made after the 12th or at the door. Contact Liz Harpold to make a reservation. We look forward to seeing you there.

  Meetings, Conventions & Events Marketing Updates  

Ketchikan Visitors Bureau offers to its members the opportunity to promote their businesses through the Welcome Bags of visitor information supplied to all conference delegates, at no cost. We strongly encourage members to provide promotional items and special offer coupons either in a generic format to be updated annually or tailor made for individual groups. With the slower winter season at hand, now is a great time to develop these items for the coming year. If you are interested in participating, please contact Meetings, Events and Groups Manager, Deb Anderson

It is estimated that the average visitor spends $248 dollars per day, on lodging, dining and shopping combined. For example, a group of 100 delegates coming to Ketchikan for a three day conference results in an economic impact of $74,400 dollars! If you are a member of a local group with statewide affiliations numbering anywhere from 5-500, please provide the contact information for the meeting planners so that enticing information on our award winning properties and the free assistance KVB offers might be provided, encouraging them to choose Ketchikan as their ultimate meeting destination.
