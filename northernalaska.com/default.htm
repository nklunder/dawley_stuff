Thank you for your interest

in Alaska's Arctic!
 

We have many exciting offerings for our guests to explore Alaska's Arctic. One such experience is our Barrow Expedition. Click on our Barrow Adventure link to learn more. The other new tour offering we are excited about is the  Polar Bear Expedition - great fall activity to look for Polar Bears in Kaktovik, Alaska (a village in the Arctic National Wildlife Refuge). A third offering is our Fairbanks by Air adventure. This morning experience is a 30 minute flight over Fairbanks and the Tanana Valley.

An Alaska travel experience would not be complete without an opportunity to experience firsthand the vast wilderness expanses and the fascinating cultures that are Alaska's Arctic.

Northern Alaska Tour Company pioneered Arctic Circle touring on Alaska's Dalton Highway. Today we take pride in being the operator of choice for those traveling in Alaska's Arctic. Our one day and multi-day excursions, available both during summer and winter, allow the traveler to experience this region in a comprehensive yet economical way.

We invite you to join us as we explore the wonders of Alaska's Arctic!
