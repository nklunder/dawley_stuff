Kotzebue One Day

Kotzebue Overnight Tour

 

Tour Summary:

 

DAY 1:

Fly north across the Arctic Circle to the Inupiat Eskimo village of Kotzebue. Receive an official Arctic Circle Certificate. Participate in a village tour for a true glimpse at life in Alaskas Arctic --- visit the local, coastal subsistence camp area, shop for traditional crafts, and visit National Park Service visitor center.   After lunch, check-in at Nullagvik Hotel.  Afternoon of free time to explore on your own OR checkout optional tour activities such as flightseeing or an in-depth visit to a local subsistence fish camp.  Overnight in Kotzebue at the all new Nullagvik Hotel.

 

DAY 2:

Transfer to airport for morning return flight to Anchorage.

 

  • DAY 1 - 6:05 am Depart Anchorage International Airport by air AS151; 7:42 am Arrive Kotzebue DAY 2 - 2:48 pm Depart Kotzebue by air AS152; 4:17 pm Arrive Anchorage
  • Flight times are subject to change
  • Tour Schedule: Mid May - Mid September

 

2012 Starting at Rate:

 

$829 / person based on double occupancy (From Anchorage)

(Add $130 for Single Supplement)
