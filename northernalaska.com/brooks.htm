Arctic Circle Overnight Adventure

An Overnight Guided Round Trip Journey By Air And Land Into Alaska's Brooks Mountain Range.

Tour Summary:

  • Fly from Fairbanks to Coldfoot, north of the Arctic Circle. The flight will provide an aerial view of the vast Alaska wilderness.
  • Explore the region on your own or participate in one or more optional tours available in Coldfoot.
  • Overnight at rustic Coldfoot.
  • Ground tour south on the rugged Dalton Highway.
  • Cross over the Arctic Circle and receive an official Arctic Circle Adventure Certificate.
  • Explore up close the amazing arctic tundra and feel the veins of ice just beneath the surface.
  • Experience the mighty Yukon River. Marvel at the majestic beauty of the northland's most famous waterway and learn of the river's storied past.
  • View the remarkable Trans Alaska Pipeline.
  • Visit the Arctic Circle Trading Post situated in the rural community of Joy, Alaska.
  • Return to Fairbanks under the light of the Midnight Sun.
  • Departs 1:00 pm on Day 1; returns 12:00 - 1:00 am on Day 2.
  • Tour Schedule: Daily May 21 - Last day for 1 pm Departure is August 19 and last day for the 5:30 am departure is September 17, 2014
 

2014 Rate: 

5:30 AM Departure $519 / person based on double occupancy. $100 Single Supplement Fee.

1:00 PM Departure $469 / person based on double occupancy. $100 Single Supplment Fee. ONLY Available up until August 19.

Beginning 20 August 2014 both departures become Arctic Circle FALL Aurora Adventure and are $499 per person based on double occupancy for a 1 night / 2 day itinerary. $100 Single Supplement Fee.  August 21 marks the start of Alaska's Aurora Season and the return of the 1 pm on August 20 returns on an Aurora ground tour.

You can easily add additional nights for $100 per person based on double occupancy making a 3 day / 2 night overnight adventure or a 4 day / 3 night adventure. 

For information on how to make a reservation, click on the link to our planning page.

 

OPTIONAL Tours for Arctic Circle Overnight Adventure

A great way to enhance your visit to the Brooks Mountain Range is by adding one or more of the following optional tours.  We do suggest if you are interested in any of the optional adventures to book them a the time of your Brooks Range Adventure reservation.

Anaktuvuk Pass Adventure - Enjoy a flight through the Brooks Mountain Range to Anaktuvuk Pass, a Nunimuit Eskimo Village surrounded by the Gates of the Arctic National Park.  ($189.00/person)

Arctic Mountain Safari - Ground tour north deeper into the Brooks Mountain Range on the Dalton Highway.  ($109.00/person)

Wiseman Wilderness Community Visit - Visit the only year-round lived in community along the Dalton Highway.  Learn of their historic past and present day lifestyle. ($59.00/person)

Koyukuk River Scenic Float - Enjoy a float down the Middle Fork Koyukuk River and enjoy the wilderness around you.  ($109.00/person)
