Midnight Sun Dinner and Float

Dine at Fairbanks' famed Two Rivers Lodge and Float the Pristine and Scenic Chena River in the Chena State Recreation Area 

 

Tour Summary:

  • Join hosts Joe & Vicki Letarte, proprietors of Wilderness Enterprises for a pleasant Alaska-style evening. Since 1982, Wilderness Enterprises has lead excursions of all  types throughout Alaska's Interior and Arctic.
  • Meet your guide at the tour departure point at the main gate of Pioneer Park in Fairbanks. Enjoy an approximately 40 minute ground tour north of Fairbanks.
  • Dining for the Midnight Sun Dinner & Float takes place at Two Rivers Lodge located in a rustic log building along Chena Hot Springs Road.   Since Territorial Days, Two Rivers Lodge has been providing local residents with the finest in Alaskan gourmet cuisine. 
  • After dinner, enjoy an approximately 1 1/2 hour leisurely float. The float takes place aboard open rafts and guests should wear comfortable walking shoes and bring a jacket.
  • The winding clear waters of the Chena River flow through the Chena State Recreation Area.  The Chena supports world class Arctic Grayling fisheries as well as runs of Yukon River king and chum salmon.  Although wildlife viewing is unpredictable; martin, beaver, otter, and bald eagles inhabit this area along with moose, black and brown bear.
  • Upon completion of the short river journey, get a glimpse of wilderness living at Joe and Vicki's shoreside log home before heading back into Fairbanks.
  • Tour Departs 5:45 pm; Returns approximately 11:00 pm. 
  • Tour Schedule: May 27 to August 31 - not available on Memorial Day or 4th of July, or on Mondays.
  • 2014 Rate: $199 per person.
