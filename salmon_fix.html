Sockeye Cycle Co. has been operating tours in Alaska and the Yukon since 1988.  We offer options for everyone including:

  • Fully supported and guided tours: we arrange all accommodations, provide all necessary equipment, carry your gear in our support van, and prepare a delicious menu of local ingredients
  • Self supported: we arrange your accommodations and provide all necessary equipment.  You carry your own gear and choose your pace.
  • Equipment rentals: we rent all gear necessary to put together your own bicycle tour.
