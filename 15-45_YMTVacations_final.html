ABOUT US
 
We started in 1967 as "Your Man Tours" arranging tours to Hawaii for veterans of foreign wars. The "Your Man" was a reference to our Polynesian guides who greeted guests at the Honolulu airport with beautiful and fragrant flower leis. Today we are known as YMT Vacations to reflect worldwide expertise in Alaska, Europe, and the domestic United States.
